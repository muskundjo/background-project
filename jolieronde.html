Welcome

La Jolie Ronde has been at the forefront of Primary Languages since 1983. A leading provider of French & Spanish language learning, for children, through extra-curricular language classes, the supply of language resources to primary schools, academies and prep schools and Educational Visits to France

Find out more about us

Why La Jolie Ronde

We offer children the opportunity to excel and enjoy a fun & positive approach to language learning through our award winning programmes, giving them confidence for when they approach languages at primary & secondary school.

Learn About Us

Award Winning Methodology

Our Programmes are centered around methodology which is based on real communication situations. Children will benefit from a structured & progressive programme and over time will begin to read & write independently, experience language learning techniques and will become accustomed to listening.

Our Method

WHAT WE DO
